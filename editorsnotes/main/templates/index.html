Editors' Notes is an open-source, web-based tool for recording, organizing, preserving, and opening access to research notes, built with the needs of documentary editing projects, archives, library special collections in mind.

How projects are using Editors' Notes

  • The Margaret Sanger Papers are researching the birth control movement in India.
  • The Elizabeth Cady Stanton & Susan B. Anthony Papers Project are collecting sources about women testing using direct action to test voting laws during reconstruction.
  • The Labadie Collection is sharing items in its collection mentioning Emma Goldman's visits to Detroit
  • The Emma Goldman Papers Project are researching the origins of the 1919 deportation of strikers in Bisbee, Arizona.
